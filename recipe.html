RECIPE

Start by boiling around 2 quarts of water in a pot. Add the radish, tomato, and onion once the water starts to boil. Continue to cook for 5 to 8 minutes. The fresh shrimp is added afterwards. Make sure to clean the shrimp before cooking. Wash the shrimp thoroughly and devein, if possible. Fresh shrimp with head attached is perfect for sinigang. Add the souring agent and then continue to cook the shrimp for 3 minutes. Add the vegetables next, except for the kangkong. Cook for 5 minutes. Usually add kangkong last. Season with patis and ground black pepper.
